We Care about


We are aiming to solve the problems faced by people with disabilities who require continuous assistance. We aim to make them self dependent in external environment. Also every disabled person isn't of means and not everyone can afford to hire a personal attendant.





What we have to offer:


Visually Impaired

Helping them in navigating around the required area.

Auditory Disability

Helping them to communicate by converting the other person's speech to text.

Vocal Disability

Helping them to communicate by converting their sign language into text.
